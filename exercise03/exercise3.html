How to Cook Plain Rice (Step-by-Step Guide)

Plain rice is a simple but essential dish that can be served with various stews, sauces, or soups. Here’s how to prepare it perfectly:

  1. Pour the rice into a bowl and rinse it with clean water 2–3 times to remove excess starch.
  2. Drain the water and set the washed rice aside.
  3. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add salt (if using) and oil or butter (optional, for a non-sticky texture).
  5. Pour the washed rice into the boiling water.
  6. Stir once to distribute the rice evenly, then reduce the heat to medium-low.
  7. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and let the rice cook for about 15–20 minutes.
  8. After 15 minutes, check if the water has been absorbed and the rice is soft.
  9. If the rice is still hard, add a little hot water (if necessary) and cook for a few more minutes.
  10. Once the rice is fully cooked, turn off the heat and let it sit covered for about 5 minutes.
  11. Use a fork to fluff the rice to separate the grains.
